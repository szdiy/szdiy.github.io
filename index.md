**深圳DIY(SZDIY)**是一个深圳本地的自由软硬件社区。
<br/>SZDIY is a free software/hardware community based in Shenzhen.

<br/>Address：Building 33 Room 516, Ke Yuan Xi, Nan Shan District, Shenzhen, China.
